I currently develop two interrelated lines of projects: (1) cognitive science and (2) cultural project development.

My research interests lay mostly in the topics of neuro- and psycholinguistics, embodied cognition, semantics and consciousness. I collaborate with Potsdam embodied cognition lab as well as Tomsk Laboratory of Linguistic Anthropology.

I am also passionate about the idea of expanding formal research methods with artistic explorations. In that direction I am currently working as an alternant on experimental research for performing arts (Elapse, Paris).

I always seek for opportunities and collaborations within or around my topics of interest, so please do not hesitate to contact me/ set up a meeting!
